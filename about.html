My Story

Having recently graduated from the CareerFoundry UI/UX Design Course, I am eager to my skills and knowledge in a professional setting.

I have a passion for art & design, as well as technology and entrepreneurship, which is why I chose to pivot from a degree in finance to UI/UX design. This is a career I can see myself continuously engaged and thriving in.

Throughout the course I have learnt the fundamentals of design and the process by which digital products are built. I have applied this knowledge practically in various case studies, which aim to replicate the design process in the workplace.

Some of my hobbies/interests include illustration, graphic design, tennis, football, martial arts, entrepreneurship and business.
